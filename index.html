I am postdoc at the Harvard University Center for the Environment for the 2022-2023 academic year; in the fall of 2023 I'll join the Economics Department at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

I received my PhD in 2022 from Berkeley ARE, where I was also a graduate student researcher at the Energy Institute at Haas.

My fields are environmental economics and public economics; I study externalities and the policies aimed at addressing them.
