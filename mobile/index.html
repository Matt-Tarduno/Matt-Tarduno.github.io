I am an assistant professor of economics at the University of Illinois at Chicago.

Prior to joining UIC, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Harvard University Center for the Environment. I received my PhD in 2022 from Berkeley ARE, where I was also a graduate student researcher at the Energy Institute at Haas.

My fields are environmental economics and public economics; I study externalities and the policies aimed at addressing them.
