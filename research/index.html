Revise and Resubmit, Journal of Political Economy Microeconomics

Student prize finalist, 11th European Meeting of the Urban Economics Association. [abstract]

Real-world congestion zones are imperfect because they charge heterogeneous road users uniform prices, and invite externality spillovers in space and time.
